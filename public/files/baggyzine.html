BAGGY ARTBOOK

SPRING/SUMMER 2025

Cover Image

Welcome to the BAGGY S/S 25 art book. This collection represents a critical examination of fashion's relationship with waste, consumption, and sustainability. Each piece is a statement—a conscious decision to elevate discarded materials into objects of desire and beauty.

Runway Image 1

In this collection, we explore the tension between luxury and waste. The silhouettes are bold, exaggerated, and deliberately provocative. Materials that were once destined for landfills have been transformed through meticulous craftsmanship.

Runway Image 2

Our design philosophy centers on radical reduction. By stripping away conventional notions of what constitutes luxury, we're left with the bare essentials: shape, texture, and movement.

Collection Detail

This art book serves as both documentation and manifesto—a call to reimagine our relationship with fashion, consumption, and waste. We invite you to join us in questioning the status quo and embracing a more sustainable future for fashion.
